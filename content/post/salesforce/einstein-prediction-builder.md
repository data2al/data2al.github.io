## Process for Building Predictions

1. Define your use case 
 - What problem is your company facing? Late payments;
 - What type of prediction will help? Predict which invoices will be paid late;
 - Can this prediction be phrased as a Yes/No or Numeric question? Will this invoice be paid late;
 - How can this prediction be used? Assign account managers to follow up;
 - How will success be measured? Percentage increase in on-time payments and percentage decrease in average number of days to pay. 
2. Plan your prediction 
3. Fill out the Einstein Prediction Builder Wizard
4. Review your scorecard
5. Enable your prediction
6. Use model in Production
