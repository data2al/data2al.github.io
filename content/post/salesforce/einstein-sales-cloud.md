## Sakesforce Email Integrations and Sync

### You want to capture meetings, emails and contacts
Storing conversations in salesforce is key to delivering SSOT and building innovation:
1. Active Listening on All Channels and Activities;
2. Capture Information;
3. Managed Sales and Service Cycles;
4. Automated Entries;
5. Timely and Relevant Insights and Next Steps.


### Salesforce has many integration options today
Retiring solutions:
Salesforce for Outlook (phase out: June 2021-2023)
- allows manually log and/or sync events and contacts
- Installed software UI plugin and sync engine
- no auto email capture

Lightning Sync (no New Orgs: Oct 2020, no definitive date for phase out)
- Cloud-based sync engine, decoupled from plug-in
- allows syncing of events and contacts
- existing customers can continue to use sync till 2023, new customers will not be able to access setup pages

Recommended Solutions:
Einstein Activity Capture (PAID)
- Requires paid 100EAC Standard Licenses with Base Sales Cloud License
- Additional Users require Inbox, Sales Cloud Einstein or High Velocity Sales License
- will be required for customers by end of 2023

- Auto email,event and contact ccapture
- Scalable capture engine on AWS


Outlook/Gmail Integration (FREE)
- Customizable UI plugin
- Manually log and update emails/events/contacts


### What to do with product retirements
- users can create records directly from salesforce
- admins may migrate users to the outlook integration(side panel) and einstein activity capture(auto sync)
- users should use the additional time to continue with evaluating their emails and syncs needed and plan to migrate to replacement product
