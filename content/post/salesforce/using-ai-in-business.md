## Using AI in Business

There are three steps to effectively utilizing AI in business.

### Decide What to Predict
The first step in using AI is to figure out what you want to achieve. You can do so by clearly defining a measurable term that you are using to predict. Without a measurable term, it is impossible to make predictions.

### Get Historical Data in Order
The second step is to obtain historical data. Using the past, we are able to predict the future. Many orgs have in-house reports, these are good starting points for AI optimization. If you report it, then you often want to predict it.

### Turn Prediction into Action
After getting the prediction results, an org can use it in a number of different ways, such as putting it on the contact record, or give the marketing team a list of customers to prioritize in reaching out. 

To summarize, in order to make the most out of AI, you need to have a concrete definition of what outcomes you want to optimize, historic data to train on, and a plan of action of how to use predictions.
