## Using AI in Business

There are three steps to effectively utilizing AI in business.

### Decide What to Predict
The first step in using AI is to figure out what you want to achieve. You can do so by clearly defining a measurable term that you are using to predict. Without a measurable term, it is impossible to make predictions.

### Get Historical Data in Order
The second step is to obtain historical data. Using the past, we are able to predict the future. Many orgs have in-house reports, these are good starting points for AI optimization. If you report it, then you often want to predict it.

### Turn Prediction into Action
After getting the prediction results, an org can use it in a number of different ways, such as putting it on the contact record, or give the marketing team a list of customers to prioritize in reaching out. 

To summarize, in order to make the most out of AI, you need to have a concrete definition of what outcomes you want to optimize, historic data to train on, and a plan of action of how to use predictions.

## Salesforce Einstein Basics Out-of-the-box applications
### Sales Cloud Einstein
- Boost win rates by prioritizing leads and opportunities most likely to convert;
- Discover pipeline trends and take action by analyzing sales cycles with pre-packaged best practices;
- Maximize time spent selling by automating data capture.

### Service Cloud Einstein
- Accerelate case resolution by auto predicting and populating fields for incoming cases
- Increase call deflection by resolving routine customer requests on real-time digital channels like web and mobile chat;
- Reduce handle time by collecting and qualifying customer info for seamless agent handoff;
- Solve issues faster by giving your agents intelligent and in-context conversation suggestions and knowledge recommendations.

### Marketing Cloud Einstein
- Uncover consumer insights and make predictions;
- Suggesting when and on which channel to reach out to customers;
- Personalized 1:1 messages and content based on consumer preferences and intent;
- Be more productive by streamlining marketing operations.

### Commerce Cloud Einstein
- Increase revenue by showing shoppers best products for them;
- Create highly visual dashboards to get a snapshot of your customer's buying patterns;
- Personalize the explicit search (search box), implicit search (storefront catalog) and category pages for every shopper, save time and bring revenue.
