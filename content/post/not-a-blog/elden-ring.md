"As I woke up at the Site of Lost Grace, I peeked across the half-abandoned battlefield in the barren hill valleys below me. I could see the Knights and Soldiers of the Golden Order patrolling the area. Their slow movements betray their ill-state of fatigue. 

Like a madman, I charged into the nearest group of soldiers, hoping to eliminate any early threats before crossing the battlefield. Halberd in one hand, Sacred Seal in the other, I circled the group like a wolf rounding up its prey. I strafed, poked, and prodded, trying to thin out the herd of men in front of me. The soldiers fought back valiantly, charging at me, swords drawn, spears ready, yet their attacks fell short as I rolled and dodged out of harm's way with ease. As another group of Soldiers closed in, I waved the seal that I was holding in my left hand, and a burst of flame consumed the Soldiers...melting armor and burning flesh. I watched in glee as the men in front of me screamed in pain and agony. 

Suddenly, a fierce blow struck me on the side of my body, sending me flying through the air. I rolled over and quickly looked up, only to see the outline of a towering knight standing before me, the next second his sword pierced my chest and I collapsed into nothingness. I wake up at the Site of Lost Grace again..."

Such was the experience of 'Elden Ring', a game designed to challenge its players with complex terrain and impossible battles. In 'Elden Ring', you start as a lowly Tarnished, an exiled member of the Round Table that once served the Queen Marika of the 'Lands In-Between'. To understand what had caused the shattering of the 'Elden Ring', you must roam the lands and piece together the clues you find. The 'Lands In-Between' is a beautiful expansive landmass, filled with castles and towers that belonged to various high lords and nobility. Fearsome Dragons soar above the skies while skilled horsemen gallop over the grasslands. A mages academy stands firmly on top of a tall rock, located in the middle of a misty lake. Its bridges were blocked with barricades and guarded by Soldiers draped in blue. The infamous Volcano Manor is marred with flame and destruction, yet underneath the hot lava and grand architecture, lies a chilling secret that is yet to be uncovered.

For those of you who wish to learn more, "KiteTales & Flex" has a youtube series detailing the lore of 'Elden Ring'. I highly recommend it.
